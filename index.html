Realistic Heterogeneous Multi-Agent RL in IsaacLab

Abstract

We present a framework for training realistic Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) environments in NVIDIA's IsaacLab. Our approach integrates heterogeneous and homogeneous agent coordination, enabling scalable training using GPU acceleration. We implement HARL algorithms alongside MAPPO, demonstrating their effectiveness in multiple environments, including collaborative bar balancing, team push, and dexterous manipulation tasks.
